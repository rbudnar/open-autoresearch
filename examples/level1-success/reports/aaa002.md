## Likely causal mechanism

The ordinal weighting penalizes distant-class mispredictions, which the baseline's plain CE treats as equivalent to any other mistake. The improvement is consistent with this account, but at Level 1 we cannot isolate it from the hybrid loss's implicit regularization (the ordinal term has a smoother gradient at the decision boundary). A Level-3 lesion test holding the hybrid coefficient at 0 vs 0.5 would disambiguate.
